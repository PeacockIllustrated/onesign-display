01

The
Challenge

Digital menus should be the easiest part of running a venue. For most teams, they're a headache.

For hospitality businesses managing digital menu boards, the daily reality is far more manual than it should be. Content lives on USB sticks. Schedules are managed by someone who “knows how to use the TV.” Updating a single price means a phone call, a design request, and a wait.

  • USB sticks and manual content swaps for every price change
  • No way to schedule breakfast, lunch and evening boards automatically
  • Zero visibility of what’s actually playing on each screen
  • Inconsistent branding across multiple locations
  • Staff who don’t know how to operate the display hardware
  • Hours lost coordinating design updates with external agencies

The Solution

Onesign Display replaces the friction between your kitchen and your screens. Upload menus to a central dashboard, assign them to screens, set your schedule — and walk away. Content plays automatically, updates instantly, and you can see every screen from anywhere.

One dashboard. Every screen. Every location.
Updated in seconds, not hours.
